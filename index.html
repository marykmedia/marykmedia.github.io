About Me

Hi, I’m Mary — a video editor and creative storyteller passionate about using media to make a positive impact. I’ve edited everything from documentaries to social campaigns, and have collaborated with brands like Apple, Disney, and NBCU. With a background in post-production, project management, and mastering, I bring both creative vision and technical know-how to every project. I’m fluent in Adobe Creative Cloud, Avid, Final Cut, DaVinci Resolve, and more, and I love collaborating with teams to take ideas from concept to polished delivery.

Beyond the technical side, I care deeply about creating meaningful content that resonates with audiences — especially work that inspires families, sparks curiosity, or drives social awareness. Whether I’m editing a sizzle reel, QC’ing TV shows, or troubleshooting a workflow, I always do my best to take an organized, reliable, and collaborative approach. I’m always curious, learning, and looking for new ways to tell a good story.
